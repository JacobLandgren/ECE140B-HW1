Key Value Proposition

The Promise

To automate the reordering and delivery of groceries.

The Benefits

  • No required work for user apart from initial account setup and scanning item's barcode before disposal.
  • Flexible ordering scheduling to integrate into the user's exisiting grocery shopping schedule.
  • Save time searching for grocery items.

Why Us

Traditional Grocery Shopping:

Saves time, energy, and gas/battery by having the grocery restocking done for you. Offers delivery for those unable to transport themselves to a grocery store.

Amazon/Whole Foods:

Access to traditional grocery stores, not exclusive to Amazon and Whole Foods. Automattically orders and fills cart when items are scanned.

Instacart, Doordash, etc.:

Automattically orders and fills cart when items are scanned. No need to search through apps for groceries and manually fill cart every time.
